About US

Welcome to sportpropa, your ultimate destination for connecting and empowering soccer enthusiasts in rural Nigeria. Our platform is designed to support communities, schools, and individual players by providing a seamless digital experience for managing and tracking soccer performances.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for soccer and a commitment to community development, sportpropa was born out of the need to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity in rural areas. In many parts of Nigeria, especially rural regions, access to resources and platforms that can showcase and develop local soccer talent is limited. We aim to change that by leveraging technology to bring communities closer and provide the tools needed for success.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between rural soccer enthusiasts and the global sports community, providing a platform that showcases talent, fosters connections, and creates opportunities for players, teams, and fans.

Our Vision

To become the premier digital platform that empowers and elevates rural sport talent, connecting them with a global audience and fostering a vibrant, inclusive sport community.

What We Offer

User Posts and Interactions:
Enable users to share updates, achievements, and experiences within the soccer community.

Competition Profile:
Create detailed profiles for local and regional competitions, highlighting schedules, results, and standout performances.

Team Profile:
Offer comprehensive profiles for teams, including history, achievements, and current rosters.

Player Profile:
Showcase individual players with detailed profiles, stats, and highlights.

Match/Game Management:
Provide tools for organizing, managing, and reporting on matches, making it easier for teams and organizers to handle logistics and records.

Why Choose Us

We believe in the power of community and the potential of every player, regardless of where they come from. Sportpropa, is dedicated to providing the tools and visibility needed to turn dreams into reality, whether it's securing a spot on a professional team, gaining recognition from scouts, or simply sharing the love of the game.
We understand the unique challenges faced by rural communities and together, we can create a future where every talent is accessible and recognized, and every community is united through the beautiful game. Whether you are a player, coach, team manager, or fan, Sportpropa.com is here to support and celebrate your passion for the beautiful game.
